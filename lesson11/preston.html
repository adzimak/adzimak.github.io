Preston City Council confirm this week’s walk-in Covid vaccination centres

By: Matt Davies

Preston City Council has announced a number of new local walk-in clinics for residents to receive their Covid-19 vaccinations. The council said these sites make getting the jab easier and more convenient, with clinicians on hand to answer questions and address any concerns people might have. Anybody over the age of 18 is now eligible to receive a Covid-19 vaccine at any clinic venue. No appointment is needed, and residents can walk in during operating hours. Hot weather has been predicted throughout the week, so arrive prepared with a drink and sunscreen as there may be a wait upon arrival.
